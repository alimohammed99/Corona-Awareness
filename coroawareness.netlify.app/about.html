What is Coronavirus?

The Corona Virus disease(COVID-19) is caused by a new strain of Coronavirus(SARS-CoV-2) that has not been identified in humans.

  • The disease is fatal
  • Spreads through droplets of an infected person
  • Social distancing is highly recommended

How To Protect Yourself

These are ways to protect yourself from Corona Virus

You should do

  • Stay at home
  • Wear mask
  • Use Sanitizer
  • Disinfect your home
  • Wash your hands
  • Frequent self-isolation

You should avoid

  • Avoid infected people
  • Avoid animals
  • Avoid handshaking
  • Aviod infected surfaces
  • Don't touch your face
  • Avoid droplets
