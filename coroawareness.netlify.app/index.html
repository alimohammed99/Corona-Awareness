What is Coronavirus?

The Corona Virus disease(COVID-19) is caused by a new strain of Coronavirus(SARS-CoV-2) that has not been identified in humans.

  • The disease is fatal
  • Spreads through droplets of an infected person
  • Social distancing is highly recommended

Symptoms

Symptoms of Corona Virus

Stay at home

Staying at home helps to prevent the spread of Corona Virus. Stay home and self-isolate.

Wear facemask

Wearing of face masks is the most important way of preventing the spread of the virus

Keep social distancing

Maintain social distancing, at least 1m from the next person

Wash your hands

Bacterias and Viruses are something we can not see with our naked eyes, so washing of hands regularly helps prevent the spread

How to Prevent Coronavirus?

These are some of the ways you can prevent the spread of this Covid-19. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization

  • Wear a mask always
  • Avoid unnecessary crowd
  • Regular bath

Symptoms of Coronavirus

Seek immediate medical atention if you have serious symptoms. Always call before visiting your doctor or health facilities.

High Fever

People develop high fever at the early stage of Corona Virus, most especially kids.

Cough

Coughing is also a symptom and can result in dry cough.

Sore Troath

This is caused by a viral infection such as cold or the flu.

Headache

Headache seems to be normal in humans but severe headache is a symptom of the disease.

Stay at home and call your doctor: When you feel you have any of the symptoms above, kindly talk to a doctor.
